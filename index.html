Synopsis

When Kim Rok Soo opens his eyes he is suddenly transported in the world of a novel.

"The Birth of a Hero".

"The Birth of a Hero" was a novel focused on the adventures of the main character, Choi Han, a high school boy who was transported to a different dimension from Earth, along with the birth of the numerous heroes of the continent. He becomes a part of that novel as the trash of the Count’s family, the family that oversaw the territory where the first village that Choi Han visits is located. The problem is that Choi Han becomes twisted after that village, and everyone in it, are destroyed by assassins. The bigger problem is the fact that this stupid trash whom he's become doesn’t know about what happened in the village and messes with Choi Han, only to get beaten to a pulp.

“…This is going to be a problem. I feel like something serious has happened to me. But it should be worth trying to make this my new life."

Characters

Cale Henituse/Kim Rok Soo

Awakens to a different world only to find out that he will soon die by the hands of the male lead that he once read from a novel. He's gonna have to do everything to stay alive and then fulfill his life long dream of becoming a slacker.

Choi Han

Transported in another world when he was only high school student back in South Korea. Trapped in the Forest of Darkness for so many years believing he was the only one of his kind in a different world. Until he met Cale Henituse.

Raon Miru

A black dragon that all he knew ever since he was born was being tortured and trapped in a cave. But on the night that he was saved he finally knew what it meant to live by staying on Cale's side.
